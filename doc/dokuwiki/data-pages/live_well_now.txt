====== Live Well Now ======

The best things in life are free.

Most important:
Nutrition
Exercise
Sleep
Education
Meditation
Nutrition
Enjoy clean, natural food and water.  Eat fresh food straight from the earth.  (Avoid packaged, processed, over-priced, addictive corporate foods.)
Exercise
Walk, run, stretch, dance, play.
Sleep
Work hard and get some exercise, and then let yourself relax, rest, rebuild.
Education
The internet is flooded with classes, tutorials, manuals, and videos, and most of it is free.  Learn something every day.
Meditation
Calm yourself, think clearly, expand your vision, see the truth.

Never be bored again.
Live well now.
