AKA Chinese cleaver or vegetable cleaver.

Classifications by thickness:

  * Thickness 2mm, steep angle - Chopping vegetables, slicing carrots paper thin, remove skin from fish.
  * Thickness 3-7mm - Versatile.  Smash garlic.
  * Thickness 8mm, shallow angle - Cutting through bones, wedging, breaking up food.


Length should be at least 7 inches.

Hardness 60 or 61.

Full tang.  Tang is joining of blade and handle.
