Modern Hinduism has its roots in The Vedas.

The Vedas are among the oldest sacred texts, dating to 1500 BC.

The Upanishads are commentary upon the Vedas, written starting in 500 BC.

Vedanta refers to the study of The Vedas as interpreted by The Upaniahads.
