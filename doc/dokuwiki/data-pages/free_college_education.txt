====== Free College Education ======

Why do you want to go to university?

1. To meet a handsome smart young girl or boy who will marry you and take care of you?
 
2. To learn a skill so you can get a high-paying job?

3. To earn a diploma so you can get a high-paying job?

4. To accomplish a specific life objective which will make the world a better place?


Things you can get from college.
1.  Knowledge and skills.
2.  Connections with peers and mentors in academia and industry.
3.  Access to high-tech equipment.
4.  A diploma.
